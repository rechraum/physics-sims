About

This simulation demonstrates the transition to chaos in a dripping faucet system using the logistic map. As you adjust the flow rate, the dripping transitions from stable periodic behavior to chaotic patterns.

Regions

Laminar (low r): Regular dripping with consistent periods — stable fixed points in the logistic map.

Period Doubling: As flow increases, the period alternates — bifurcations appear as branch splits on the diagram.

Chaos (high r): Irregular, unpredictable dripping — sensitive dependence on initial conditions.
